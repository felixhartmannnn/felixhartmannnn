## About Me

I'm a researcher focused on the intersection of **window managers**, **large language model reasoning**, and **autonomous agents**. I build and share open-source tools, conduct evaluations, and explore experimental systems that push the boundaries of what AI can do.

- 🔬 Researching WMs, LLM reasoning, and autonomous agents
- 🛠️ Sharing open-source research, evaluations, and experimental systems
- 📚 Committed to transparent, reproducible research

---

## Current Focus

- **Window Managers**: Research into compositor architecture, tiling algorithms, and input handling
- **LLM Reasoning**: Investigating how language models reason, plan, and execute complex tasks
- **Autonomous Agents**: Building agents that perceive, decide, and act with minimal human intervention
- **Open-Source Evaluation**: Developing benchmarks and evaluation frameworks for AGI
